Purpose

International Day of Families is celebrated on May 15th every year. It was created by the United Nations to raise awareness about the challenges families face all around the world. Each year, a different theme is selected to focus on, such as poverty or equality. The most recent theme, in 2024, was “Families and Climate Action”. Furthermore, International Day of Families encourages people to celebrate diversity all around the world no matter the background. Therefore, it is a time to strengthen family bonds and recognize their role in society. Since families are so important, this day helps us remember to work together for a better future. It was proclaimed by the UN General assembly in 1993.
